Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Marine Manager is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, sell or share your personal data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Marine Manager stores the following data solely to provide app functionality:

  • Vessel and fleet information you enter (names, registrations, technical data)
  • Maintenance records, observations and tasks you create
  • Photos you attach to records
  • Sea trial routes and GPS coordinates recorded during trials
  • Running hours and cost entries

2. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — using iOS local storage (UserDefaults)
  • In your private iCloud account — using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage for sync across your devices

We have no access to your iCloud data. It is encrypted and managed entirely by Apple.

3. Sign In with Apple

Marine Manager uses Sign In with Apple for authentication. When you sign in:

  • Apple provides us with a unique user identifier
  • Your name and email are optional and only used to personalise your experience
  • We never store your Apple ID password
  • You can sign out at any time from the app settings

4. Location Data

Marine Manager requests location access for two purposes:

  • Fetching local weather conditions for sea trial records
  • Recording GPS routes during sea trials

Location data is stored only on your device and in your private iCloud. It is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Camera and Photos

Marine Manager requests camera and photo library access to allow you to attach photos to maintenance records and observations. Photos are stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud only.

6. Weather Data

Marine Manager fetches weather information from Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com), a free and open-source weather API. This service receives your GPS coordinates to return local weather data. No personal information is transmitted.

7. Analytics and Tracking

Marine Manager does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks. We do not track your behaviour across apps or websites.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade or share your data with any third parties. Your fleet data is entirely private.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual records within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device (removes all local data)
  • Signing out and deleting your iCloud data from iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage

10. Children's Privacy

Marine Manager is intended for professional maritime use and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new vessel? +
Tap the + button on the Fleet screen and fill in your vessel's details — name, IMO number, flag, type and any technical specifications. All information is saved locally and synced to your iCloud automatically.
How do I log a maintenance record? +
Select your vessel, go to the Maintenance tab and tap +. Choose the equipment, enter the work done, date, running hours and any notes. You can also attach photos directly from your camera or photo library.
How do running hours work? +
Running hours are tracked per piece of equipment. Each time you log a maintenance entry you can update the current hours. The app uses these to calculate when the next service interval is due and highlights overdue items automatically.
How do I record a sea trial? +
Go to the Sea Trials section and tap Start Trial. The app will record your GPS route, speed and weather conditions automatically. When the trial is complete, tap Stop to save the record with the full route and performance data.
Is my data backed up? +
Yes. Marine Manager syncs your data to your private iCloud account automatically. If you sign in with the same Apple ID on another device, your fleet data will be available there too. You can also back up your device via iCloud Backup or Finder/iTunes.
Can I use the app on multiple devices? +
Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID on all your devices and your fleet data will sync across them automatically via iCloud Key-Value Storage.
How do I delete a vessel or record? +
Swipe left on any vessel or record in the list to reveal the Delete button, or open the record and use the delete option in the menu. Deleted data is removed from all your devices via iCloud sync.
The weather isn't loading — what should I check? +
Make sure you have granted Marine Manager location access in iOS Settings → Marine Manager → Location → While Using the App. Also check that you have an active internet connection — weather data is fetched from Open-Meteo in real time.
